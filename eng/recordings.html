Recordings are a common element in the life of any musician: from the famous (and now outdated) "demos" to somewhat more elaborate products.

Belonging to this latter category are two works in which I directly participated: Quebrando el silencio ("Breaking the Silence"), with the group Wayra (Gran Canaria, Spain, 1996), and Vientos de tierra de vientos ("Winds of Land of Winds", Córdoba, Argentina, 2007), a self-production in which I acted as a multi-instrumentalist.

Alongside those earlier works, I'm currently developing new recordings as part of Instrumentarium's sound archive, including One, Bare and Raw: sets of solo pieces of my own composition, written for specific instruments and materials, as a way to test techniques, tunings, and aesthetics in practice. New items will appear here as they are completed.
