Home > Publications

 

This section provides an account of my intellectual production in the framework of the Instrumentarium project, on music and musical instruments. It includes:

  Books in Spanish (see).
  Books in English (see).
  Articles and compilations in Spanish (see).

All of my written work, organized into categories, is available for free and open access in the "Publicaciones" folder of my space at Google Drive (subfolder "Music").

All content is copyrighted and distributed under a Creative Commons license (see copyright and conditions).

 

Open-access archives

My work is uploaded to Archive.org and the Argentinean open access repository Acta Académica. A handful of those texts are also available in the open access archive DLIST, from the University of Arizona (USA). And although in the past, I stored and presented them on E-LIS, they were removed from there around 2016 due to strong differences with the structure and management model of that space.

 

Platforms

I present my publications on three platforms of popular use, albeit somewhat debated for their work models. These are Issuu, Scribd and Calaméo.

Furthermore, I distribute them through various academic and professional networks.
