This section gathers short, self-contained fragments —or shards— about musical instruments from across the world. Each piece is the result of research and observation which, due to its brevity and format, does not lend itself to full academic articles, yet is prepared with the same rigor and attention to detail.

These shards explore historical, technical, cultural, and sonic aspects of instruments, offering small windows into the vast diversity of the world's musical heritage. For dissemination or use as a source, the same citation rules that apply to academic work must be observed.

From time to time, the complete set of shards will be compiled into thematic series and published as digital books through the imprint Wayrachaki Editora, and included in the "Publications" section.
