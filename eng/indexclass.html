The research-action project Musical Instruments Classification (MusIC) combines a critical reassessment of the Hornbostel & Sachs system with the development of a new energetic–acoustic taxonomy grounded in the understanding of instruments as documentary and epistemic objects.

In addition to the classification scheme and its related texts, the project includes:

  • Records for individual instruments, describing their structure, uses, contexts, and energetic–acoustic profiles.
  • An organological index (this page), providing structured access to instruments and classes through multiple entry points: instrument name, excitation type, material, cultural area, or classification number.

The index is under active development and will be expanded progressively as the new taxonomy and the instrument records are consolidated.
