The action-research project Musical Instruments Classification (MusIC) develops a new energetic–acoustic taxonomy for sound-producing objects, grounded in excitation, acoustic behaviour, and material interaction, and aligned where relevant with the Hornbostel & Sachs framework.

This page will host individual instrument records. Each record will integrate:

  • Organological and structural description.
  • Contexts of use, performance practices, and cultural significance.
  • Energetic–acoustic classification within the new decimal system.
  • Links to related instruments, classes, and theoretical categories.

The records are being prepared and will be published progressively as the classification tables and index expand.

For an overview of the project, see the classification scheme and the organological index.
