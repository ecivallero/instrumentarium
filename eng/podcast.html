Home > Podcast Sounds and silences

 

Sounds and silences is an upcoming podcast series woven into the heart of Instrumentarium. It will explore traditional musical instruments not only as sound-makers but as memory-keepers — artifacts that encode territory, ancestry, silence, resistance, and rhythm.

In each episode, I'll trace the life of a sound: from forest or desert to archive, from ritual to ruin. These are not just stories about instruments — they are narratives about the hands that shaped them, the tongues that named them, and the silences they survived.

Through field recordings, commentary, interviews, and sonic essays, the podcast will serve as an audible extension of this digital platform: a space to listen not only to music, but to the knowledge, politics, and identities that resonate within it.
