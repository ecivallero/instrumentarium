Musical Instruments Classification (MusIC)

The action-research project Musical Instruments Classification (MusIC) combines a critical review of the Hornbostel & Sachs system with the design of a new energetic–acoustic taxonomy. The project addresses structural limitations in the existing framework — including its morphological bias, its treatment of hybrid and electroacoustic instruments, and its semantic constraints.

The resulting model approaches instruments as documentary and epistemic objects and organizes them through a modular decimal structure based on excitation, material interaction, and acoustic behaviour. The project is currently ongoing, and during 2026 it will progressively release the corresponding academic articles, classification tables, theoretical classes, and technical documentation.

The system is being implemented through classification tables, individual instrument records, and an organological index, all of which will expand as the documentation becomes public.
