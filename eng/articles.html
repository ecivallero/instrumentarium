Home > Publications > Articles and compilations on music

 

The following articles and compilations are texts presented in different platforms, digital spaces or magazines (academic and/or popularization). All of them, published mainly in digital format (.pdf), present original bibliographic research related to organology and ethnomusicology, and are distributed for free reading, according to Instrumentarium's copyright policies.
